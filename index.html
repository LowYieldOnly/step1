How this works

What this is. A day-by-day Step 1 plan built around your exam date and the resources you use. Everything stays on your device — no account, nothing uploaded.

Today. Your home base: exactly what to watch (Boards & Beyond), annotate (First Aid), unsuspend (AnKing), and drill (UWorld) for the day — plus Pathoma and Sketchy if you use them. Check each off as you finish.

Unsuspending Anki cards. Tap Copy Anki search on the AnKing line, then paste into Anki’s Browse search bar. Exactly that day’s cards appear — select all and unsuspend.

The other tabs. Schedule is the full calendar. Mastery shows where you’re weak. Pace tells you whether you’re on track.

Change anything. Tap ⋮ → Edit my plan to change your exam date, hours, days off, resources, or study order — the schedule rebuilds instantly.

Falling behind? On Pace, tap Re-flow remaining days to re-date everything from today, or turn on auto-shift in Edit my plan.

New device? ⋮ → Backup saves a small file; Restore it on another computer to carry your progress. (Progress is saved per browser.)

Free to share — send classmates the link and each person sets up their own plan. Your progress stays yours alone.
